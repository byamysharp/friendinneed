How we got started

I started thinking about doing home care back in 1998 when my father’s mother took ill. Seeing her placed in a nursing care facility because there was no space or time to care for her broke my heart. I was just 15 years-old at the time and vowed to put myself in a position to help those who are unable to help themselves.

Hearing the stories on the news about seniors and those with disabilities being abused helps me to not only research my community's needs but be the change I want to see as well.

A Friend In Need Home Care is committed to providing superior personal care services at the lowest possible cost while maintaining the highest level of respect, dignity, and appreciation for our customers and their family members.
